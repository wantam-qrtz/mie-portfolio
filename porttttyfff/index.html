About Me

Who Am I?

I'm Douglas Simiyu Burare, a Kenyan cybersecurity enthusiast and front-end developer based in Nairobi. My journey in technology began with a fascination for how things work in the digital space, which evolved into a passion for creating secure and user-friendly web experiences.

With a strong foundation in front-end technologies and a growing expertise in cybersecurity, I bridge the gap between development and security. I believe that security should be integrated from the initial stages of development, not added as an afterthought.

When I'm not coding or researching security vulnerabilities, you can find me contributing to open-source projects, attending tech meetups, or mentoring aspiring developers in my community.
